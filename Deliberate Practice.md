A regimen of training activities (ideally, designed by an expert for the learner specifically) to improve specific aspects of your performance. [Daily Deliberate Practice over about ten years is the best predictor of expertise, better than talent, intelligence, and physical aptitude in every field (except height in some sports)](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/5318154_Expert_and_Exceptional_Performance_Evidence_of_Maximal_Adaptation_to_Task_Constraints).
## Key Components:
### Specific, well-defined goals.
### Focused attention and effort.
### [Immediate, informative feedback](Using%20Feedback).
### Repetition and refinement.
### Development of mental representations of the skill.
"Expert performers acquire mechanisms for internally representing their current situation as a precondition for planning, reasoning, anticipating, and controlling motor production."
